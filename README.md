# Scroll-with-the-Flow
## Project Background
- This is my senior project that I did with my friend, Khemika. We realized that many university students normally face procrastination, anxiety from studying, and also emotional exhaustion. And the behavior that happened in this era is problematic smartphone use. As we want to know whether those are actually related, led to this project.
- This project analyze the data in order to study in the influence of Emotional Exhaustion towards Academic Procrastination which has Mindless scrolling as a mediator, moderated by Academic Anxiety
- Assumptions:
  1. Mindless Scrolling is the mediator of the relation between Emotional Exhaustion and Academic Procrastination, with positive indirect effect.
  2. Academic Anxiety is the moderator which strengthened the positive relation between  Emotional Exhaustion and Mindless Scrolling.
     
## Measurements
We collected data for each variable by using likert-scale questionnaires from the past paper, translated in Thai language

## Summary
- According to the simple mediation analysis, the result supports the first assumption: Mindless Scrolling is a complete mediator of the relation between Emotional Exhaustion and Academic Procrastination, with positive indirect effect. That is, participants with high emotional exhaustion scores tend to have high mindless scrolling scores and high academic procrastination scores. 
- The relation of Emotional Exhaustion and Academic Procrastination mediated by Mindless Scrolling is not, significantly, moderated by Academic Anxiety.
